My Favorite Terraria Content Mods

I play this game too much and so I went to look at mods that added new content as if it was free downloadable content. I found a couple that I really loved; Here they are.

  1. Currently the most downloaded mod in the tModLoader workshop in Steam, and for good reason as well.

    The mod adds more hours into a game, which already takes a ton of hours to beat, and includes content and game progression even after beating the base game's final boss.

    Calamity adds tons of new bosses, biomes, thousands of items, NPCs, and even harder difficulty options.

  2. Thorium feels as if the developers of Terraria itself made an update to the game.

    Along with adding more bosses, even more items than Calamity, and NPCs, there are also 3 new classes added in this mod.

  3. Take Terraria and turn it into Dark Souls, along with adding an unnecessarily large crafting tree.

    Fargo's Soul Mod's primary features include adding a difficulty mode which almost completely changes the way enemies and bosses work, and with that, it also adds numerous crafting recipes which all build up to a single, powerful accessory at the end of the game.

    I have played all of these mods twice or thrice now, with Calamity being the most repeatedly played mod by me. I do not even know how many times I have replayed that mod.

    I hope that, if you have Terraria, and if you have enoughb experience in that game, that you try out these mods (I do not recommend trying out Fargo's Soul Mod first).

    Thank you for reading!
